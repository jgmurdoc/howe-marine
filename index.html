Our Story

Serving the Inland Waterway Since 1938

Howe Marine sits directly on the Indian River between Burt and Mullett Lakes — the heart of Michigan's famous Inland Waterway. For over eight decades we've been the trusted stop for Northern Michigan boaters.

Covered and open slips up to 38′, indoor and outdoor storage, pontoon rentals, factory-authorized engine repair, gas dock, deep-water launch ramp, and a fully stocked Ship Store.
